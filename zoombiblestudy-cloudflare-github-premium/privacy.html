Your privacy matters

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

ZoomBibleStudy.com collects only the information you voluntarily submit through the website, such as your name and email address, so we can deliver the resources you requested and communicate with you about the live Zoom Bible discussion.

Information we collect

When you use the signup form, we may collect your first name, email address, the page or form you used, and basic technical information processed by our website or email provider.

How we use your information

  • To send the free Steps to Christ ebook.
  • To send invitations and information about the live Zoom Bible discussion.
  • To share occasional related Christian resources and encouragement.
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the website.

How we protect your information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Information may be processed by trusted service providers used to host the website, deliver email, manage forms, or conduct the Zoom discussion. Those providers operate under their own privacy terms.

Your choices

You may unsubscribe from email communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link included in an email. You may also request access to or deletion of your information through the contact method provided in the emails you receive.

Cookies and analytics

This version of the website does not include advertising trackers. If analytics or additional third-party tools are added later, this policy should be updated to reflect those services.

Questions

For privacy questions, reply to an email you receive from Zoom Bible Study or use the ministry contact information included in that message.
